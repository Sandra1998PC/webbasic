Name:Khasakinte Ithihasam

Author:OV Vijayan

Pages:150

Published in 1969, it has undergone more than 50 reprints since, making it one of the biggest bestselling books in Asia. The novel was translated into English by the author himself in 1994, titled The Legends of Khasak. It has also been translated into French and German. The story traces the journey of the protagonist Ravi, who is tormented by the guilt of having an illegitimate love affair with his stepmother.
